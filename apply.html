How to Apply for Our Programs!

Application

To receive a copy of the application form, contact us either by email at admin@nature.afterschool.org or through our Contact Us page. We recommend that you have on hand documents about identification, health insurance, any financial assistance and any special requirements that the child may have.

Financial Assistance

Although we have limited financial assistance options through the school, you can email us at financial.assistance@nature.afterschool.org to see what programs and/or options may be available to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept DSS and/or WDI?

Yes, we accept subsidies for families in need. For information, please contact us at financial.assistance@nature.afterschool.org

How do you handle emergencies?

In case of minor emergencies such as bruises or scrapes, we have first-aid kits on hand. For anything more severe, we call 911 (which is why we ask for health insurance information).

Can I drop my kids in for a full day without notice?

No; unfortunately we do not at present provide daycare services to our students.

What are your after-school hours?

The Afterschool is open from Monday through Friday 4PM-6PM during our open season.
