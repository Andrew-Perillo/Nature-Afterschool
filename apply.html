How to Apply for Our Programs!

Program Requirements

  • In order to join the program, your child must be between the ages of 8-18.
  • Each program lasts for only two hours; someone must pick up your child at the end of the day.
  • The child must use our standardized equipment.
  • The child must bring their own food, if necessary.
