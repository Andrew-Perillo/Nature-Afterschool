How to Apply for Our Programs!

Program Requirements

  • In order to join the program, your child must be between the ages of 8-17.
  • Each program lasts for only two hours; someone must pick up your child at the end of the day.
  • The child must use our standardized equipment.
  • The child must bring their own food, if necessary.

Dates and Deadlines

At the Nature Afterschool, we have courses running the entire summer long. Here is a list of dates for our upcoming classes:

  • Hiking: May 15th - June 10th
  • Venture: June 15th - July 10th
  • Horseback Riding: July 15th - August 15th
