At Nature Afterschool, our children head into the woods and off the trails where they’re meeting plants and animals, learning wilderness skills, creating natural crafts, and building deep connections with friends and inspiring mentors.

Nature Afterschool’s vision for every person from every walk of life to have the opportunity to explore, play, and learn in nature and while building strong connections to themselves, each other and the nature.

Nature Afterschool Cultivates Character:

  • Gratitude & Humility
  • Awareness & Empathy
  • Honesty & Integrity
  • Quiet Mind & Self Control
  • Curiosity, Passion & Purpose
  • Optimism & Vitality
  • Confidence, Courage & Agency
  • Service & Leadership
  • Grit & Resilience
  • Sense of Belonging

Nature Afterschool’s programs are designed to remember and return to our natural human blueprint to form strong connections to self, each other and the Earth. We offer unique, empowering indoor and outdoor experiences that help us each grow physically, emotionally and socially. Likewise, at a time when our planet requires diligent and proactive care, Nature Afterschool’s programs foster gratitude, respect, and a renewed commitment to taking care of the nature.

Since our founding in 2021, Nature Afterschool has engaged thousands of people thru capital region (NY) in empowering nature immersion programs with inspiring mentors.

Our Mission: Nature Afterschool leads transformative nature immersion experiences that cultivate character, confidence, passion and perseverance in Capital-Hudson Valley New York’s youth.

About Us: Located along our Capital-Hudson Valley Region of New York’s, Nature Afterschool is a for-profit that runs nature-based programs for children and teens.

About Our Programs: Nature Afterschool programs draw on a broad spectrum of teachings ranging from native technologies to the natural sciences. Our programs offer adventure and fun, skills and crafts, awareness games, and story and song, facilitated by our four founders and entrepreneurs in coding Tahseen Haq, Andrew Perillo, Ysabel Barreto and Manuel Blas as well as our very own mentors. They foster individual and group awareness and a deeper connection to nature.

Core Practices in Nature Afterschool Programs

All Nature Afterschool programs seek to offer experiences that strengthen nature awareness and knowledge of place in a joyful and inspiring way.

Outlined below are common themes to help guide the curricular flow and to help support the intuitive structure of all Nature Afterschool programs. Much of the distillation below is inspired by Jon Young and can be found in greater detail in his recently published book, Coyote’s Guide to Connecting with Nature. All program coordinators, instructors, board members and volunteers are encouraged to incorporate these focal points into their endeavors. May these core practices help guide us all as we bring together our unique talents, experiences and perspectives into one community basket.
